How to download QR


To download your QR code using the right-click method, first generate the QR by entering your text or URL. Once the QR code appears, move your mouse over the image, right-click on it, and choose "Save image as..." from the menu. After that, select your preferred folder, give the file a name if needed, and finally click Save to store the QR code on your device.
